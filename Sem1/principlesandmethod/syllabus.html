Methods and orientations.
Experiential learning of John Dewey
Developing competencies and outcomes
Developing tasks and activities

Understanding Students in Educational Instruction - 5 hours

Instructional media

Classroom Management - 5 hours
Management for efficiency and management for equity

Ensuring the success for all reaching out to all

Work as context for learning - 5 hours
Complexity of work and learning
Different forms of work-based learning
Developing work-based learning modules

Unit 7:
Transversal Skills for teachnical and vocational eduction - 4 hours
The 4 C framework - Communication, Critical Thinking, Creativity and Collaboration
The blend of soft and hard skills
